# Proposal — satellite-source-audit

## Why

Changes 1–4 built the satellite as a **sensor, not judge**: the Worker re-derives every conclusion and keeps irreversible actions human-gated. The credo also promised the Worker would *quarantine bad sources through the pipeline*, and `satellite/spec.md` carries a standing SHALL that a source repeatedly failing validation be *quarantinable through the pipeline (surfaced and rejectable)*. Today the only lever is `revokeIngestKey` (whole-machine), and reject *reasons* evaporate in the sync HTTP response — the operator has no rejection rear-view mirror and no per-source lever. This change realizes that clause and its audit trail: a rejection ledger, a per-`{kind,source}` reliability signal, and a reversible, operator-confirmed per-source quarantine. It DROPS the credo's *sample against ground truth* clause — the satellite exists to reach sources the Worker cannot independently verify, so a ground-truth check would be theater; we audit operational health, trusting the satellite's honesty.

## What Changes

- **A rejection ledger (`satellite_rejections`).** A new D1 table (accessed only via `src/db.ts`, throw-free) recording each REJECTED observation: `{ id, tenant, key_id, kind, source, origin: local|worker, reason, provenance, count, rejected_at }`. Fed by (1) all three arms of `intakeObservations` on a Worker-side reject (`origin: worker`), and (2) satellite-reported LOCAL rejects (`origin: local`, below). Rolling-window prune mirroring `pruneIngestPushes`/`pruneStaleOrderLists`. This gives the operator the rejection rear-view mirror they entirely lack — reject reasons that today vanish into the sync response become durable and surfaceable.
- **An agent-readable read (`read_satellite_rejections`).** A `read_*`-style MCP read over the ledger, mirroring the `reconcile_errors` → `read_reconcile_errors` precedent, so the agent (and the operator) can inspect a source's recent rejections and relay the specific defect.
- **Local-reject reporting on the delivery envelopes.** The loudest breakage — a broken adapter emitting malformed or judgment-smuggling data — is caught LOCALLY by `validateSaleEmit`/`validateOrderEmit`/the recipe adapter's contract check and dropped before the wire, so a Worker-side-only ledger is blind to it. The satellite reports a compact **local-reject summary** — per `{ reason_category, count, sample }` — via an **additive, OPTIONAL** field on the three delivery envelopes (the push batch, the pull-channel results POST, the order-receipt POST). Additive+optional ⇒ stays `contract_version: "v2"` (a satellite that omits it is unaffected). The Worker records each summary entry into the ledger with `origin: local`.
- **A reliability signal.** A per-`{kind, source}` **compute-on-read** rollup over the ledger + accepted-counts: acceptance rate, validation/plausibility-fail rate, staleness. Surfaced as a new **"quality" dimension** on the existing **Discovery › Satellites** admin page, beside the fresh/stale recency it already shows. The "source" key is composite `{kind, source-string}` — recipe → the feed/site URL, sale/order → the store slug.
- **A quarantine lever.** A **reversible, per-source, Worker-side reject flag**, operator-**confirmed** (a recommendation is surfaced when a source crosses a health threshold; the operator toggles it — NOT auto-disable). Since the Worker is outbound-only, enforcement is Worker-side: a quarantined source's future observations are rejected at intake and logged to the ledger with `origin: worker, reason: quarantined`. It complements (does not replace) `revokeIngestKey`, and finally implements the standing "surfaced and rejectable through the pipeline" spec text as a per-source lever rather than a whole-machine one.

**Threat model — fallible-only, not adversarial.** Ground-truth sampling is dropped by decision: the satellite reaches API-less stores the Worker CANNOT independently reach, so a "verify a store claim against ground truth" check would be half-truth theater. We trust the satellite's honesty and audit only operational **health** (breakage: a DOM change, a rotted adapter, an expired session flooding garbage). Blast radius is per-household (ledger + quarantine entries are tenant/household-scoped), so this is the operator's own health tool, not a cross-tenant security boundary.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- **`satellite-source-audit`** — the sensor-health audit + source-quarantine arm: the `satellite_rejections` ledger fed by every Worker-side reject across all three intake arms; the additive local-reject summary on the three delivery envelopes (recorded `origin: local`); the per-`{kind, source}` reliability rollup (compute-on-read: acceptance/fail rate + staleness); the reversible operator-confirmed per-source quarantine enforced at intake; the agent-readable `read_satellite_rejections`; the fallible-only threat model (no ground-truth sampling). Owns its own accounting substrate (the ledger + a sale/order accept-tally) and reuses `ingest_pushes` for the recipe denominator it already provides.

### Modified Capabilities

- **`satellite`** — the standing "The Worker trusts a satellite's validated outputs, never its process" requirement's quarantine SHALL becomes an **implemented mechanism**: a repeatedly-failing source is surfaced in the ledger + the reliability signal and is **quarantinable per-source** (a reversible operator-confirmed Worker-side reject), complementing whole-machine key revocation. The push-wire requirement notes the v2 envelope MAY additionally carry the optional, additive `local_rejects` operational-health summary, which keeps `contract_version: "v2"`.

## Impact

- **One D1 migration.** `packages/worker/migrations/d1/0039_satellite_rejections.sql` (next available) creates `satellite_rejections` (the ledger) and a small `satellite_source_stats` accept-tally + a `satellite_quarantine` flag table (or equivalent — see design.md), all accessed via a new `src/satellite-audit-db.ts` → `src/db.ts` (throw-free). No backfill; the tables start empty (the whole satellite spine is dormant in production — see design.md spike).
- **Shared contract.** `packages/contract` gains the optional `local_rejects` summary shape (defined once), added additively to the push batch, the pull-channel results, and the order-receipt envelopes. `CONTRACT_VERSION` stays `"v2"`.
- **Worker.** `intakeObservations` writes a ledger row per Worker-side reject (all three arms) and checks the per-source quarantine flag before accepting; the pull-results and order-receipt handlers thread the optional local-reject summary into the ledger; the reliability rollup extends `readSatelliteLiveness`; a ledger prune joins the `scheduled()` phase-1 reap beside `pruneStaleOrderLists`. All D1 via `src/db.ts`.
- **Satellite package.** The scan/order loops and the recipe push assemble the compact local-reject summary from the already-collected `ScanOutcome.rejected` / order-emit rejects / recipe-emit rejects and attach it to the outbound envelope. `satellite-version` bumped (contract + satellite touched).
- **Admin panel (`/admin/**`).** The Satellites page gains a quality column, per-source rejection detail, and a quarantine toggle (an island mutation over a new `/admin/api/*` route) — so this ships its **companion Claude Design** prompt + updated bundle and its **Playwright** coverage (page objects/specs under `admin/visual/`, the blocking `admin-ui` job + sticky screenshot comment).
- **Persona.** `AGENT_INSTRUCTIONS.md` gains a note that `read_satellite_rejections` explains why a satellite's contributions aren't landing (validated conversationally); the plugin skills are rebuilt from source.
- **Docs (lockstep).** `docs/SCHEMAS.md` (the ledger + accept-tally + quarantine tables and the additive `local_rejects` wire field), `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md` (the sensor-audit/quarantine surface, the fallible-only threat model, "Model identity: none"), `docs/TOOLS.md` (`read_satellite_rejections`).
